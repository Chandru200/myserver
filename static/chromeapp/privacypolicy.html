Privacy Policy

ProductivityMaster is a browser extension that helps users to manage their tasks, stay organized, and maintain healthy web browsing habits. In order to provide these features, ProductivityMaster uploads task details, URL details, and time to our servers.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information from you:

  • Task details: This includes the name, description, and due date of your tasks.
  • URL details: This includes the URLs of the websites that you visit.
  • Time: This includes the time that you spend on each website.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to provide the features of ProductivityMaster. For example, we use your task details to help you to manage your tasks and track your progress. We use your URL details to help you to identify and avoid websites that are distracting or unproductive. We use your time data to help you to understand and manage your online time.

How do we protect your information?

We take steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use a variety of security measures, including encryption and access controls.

Who do we share your information with?

We do not share your information with any third parties without your consent.

Your choices

You can choose to opt out of having your information collected and used by ProductivityMaster. To do this, you can uninstall the extension.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated privacy policy on our website.
